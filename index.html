About Us

The Polo Lone Riders Saddle Club is a not for profit, community-based, family-oriented organization comprised of members and volunteers who are dedicated to education and skill development in all disciplines pertaining to equine activities.

â€‹Founded in 1998, the Lone Riders Saddle Club developed and currently supports the 150'x300' Circle T outdoor arena at Stagecoach Park just south of Polo, Missouri, on Highway 13.

Our family-based membership meets on the first non-holiday Monday of each month. We welcome new members interested in horseback riding and related activities, as well as those who just enjoy the sport.

We hope you'll come out and join us - whether as a participant or spectator - young or young at heart!

Young or Old, Beginner or Seasoned Professional, come join us at the best arena in northwest Missouri.

Want to join in on the fun? Join the Polo Lone Riders Saddle Club for just $10 a year for an individual and $20 a year for a family. The club meets on the 1st Monday of each month to discuss upcoming events and club business. The annual fee grants free admission to all saddle club events as well as a vote in club business.
